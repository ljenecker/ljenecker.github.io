Partnership Models

Two structured pathways for corporates and government to partner with IEGF and drive measurable impact.

CSI Alignment

Align your Corporate Social Investment strategy with IEGF's on-the-ground programmes. We provide the implementation infrastructure; you provide the investment and strategic direction.

  • ✓Direct alignment with BBBEE and CSI reporting requirements
  • ✓Measurable impact metrics and regular reporting
  • ✓Community-level visibility and brand association
  • ✓Co-branded programme delivery

Supplier Development

Develop emerging enterprises within IEGF's network into viable suppliers for your value chain. We prepare businesses for formal market entry and procurement readiness.

  • ✓Pipeline of vetted, trained micro and small enterprises
  • ✓Compliance and quality readiness programmes
  • ✓Market access facilitation and mentorship
  • ✓Enterprise development spend tracking
