Who We Are

A global forum for people-led development, transforming the informal economy into a powerful engine of progress.

A Global Forum for People-Led Development

The Informal Economy Global Forum (IEGF) is a people-driven global platform working directly at community level to unlock opportunity, strengthen resilience, and build sustainable local economies. We are transforming the informal economy into a powerful engine of social and economic progress, because the people who operate within it are the heartbeat of national development.

IEGF brings together countries, communities, and everyday change makers who share one mission: to uplift individuals, support businesses, and empower communities through practical systems that create measurable impact.

We design and implement structured development pathways that connect people to skills training, market access, financial inclusion, supply chains, and community-based support networks. These systems ensure that growth is not accidental ‚Äî it is planned, supported, and scalable.

With a focused rollout across Africa from 2026 to 2028, IEGF is establishing national leadership structures in every operating country to ensure implementation is locally driven, culturally aligned, and impact-focused. Our work is rooted in grassroots realities while connected to regional and global opportunities, creating a bridge between survival and sustainable economic participation.

Our Vision

To transform and empower individuals, businesses and communities to build self-sustaining livelihoods and contribute to national development across emerging markets globally.

Our Mission

To empower people to grow through access to skills, market opportunities, financial readiness, community-based empowerment systems and cross-border collaboration that transforms us from survival to sustainability.
