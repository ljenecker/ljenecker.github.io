Support

Get help, schedule mentoring sessions, and connect with your support network.

The IEGF Market Access Program is a structured 18-month business development and sustainability program designed to build compliant, financially stable, operationally sound, and socially responsible businesses across multiple sectors. It covers compliance, branding, operations, mentoring, expansion, and community impact.

Each stage spans 3 months, making the full program 18 months. Stage 1 (Months 1-3) covers Compliance & Financial Wellbeing, Stage 2 (Months 4-6) covers Branding & Infrastructure, and so on through all 6 stages.

Each stage has specific member costs. Stage 1 includes company registration fees, tax clearance, and accounting setup. Stage 2 covers branding and digital setup. Later stages include operational tools, expansion capital, and CSI contributions. Sponsors may subsidise some costs.

Funding readiness training is provided in Stage 1, along with assistance submitting grant applications. Additional funding guidance is available in Stage 5 for members preparing to scale their operations.

All participants who complete the 18-month program automatically qualify for entry into the IEGF Awards & Recognition Program. The Awards Gala recognises top performers, celebrates compliance excellence, highlights growth milestones, and connects members with investors and partners.

The program uses a hybrid delivery model combining online and face-to-face sessions. Communication happens through WhatsApp sector groups, and all training modules and resources are available through this dedicated membership portal.
