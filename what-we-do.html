Task Forces

Sector Development Task Forces

Our Sector Development Task Forces are the engines that drive industry growth within the IEGF ecosystem. Made up of experienced leaders, practitioners, and sector specialists, these task forces design and implement structured development programs that strengthen industries from the ground up.

From agriculture to technology and everything in between, we provide infrastructure support, sector-wide skills development, mentorship, and collaborative growth strategies that enable businesses to scale and sectors to thrive collectively.

With focused projects running from 6 to 36 months, we create sustainable impact that positions entire sectors to compete, innovate, and work seamlessly with others. If you're ready to help shape the future of your industry, this is where transformation begins.

Grassroots Impact Connect

Where Change Begins

Grassroots Impact Connects are the heartbeat of the Informal Economy Global Forum (IEGF). These local spaces bring opportunity directly into communities, creating face-to-face, accessible, practical, and people-centered support where it is needed most.

Through skills development, business guidance, financial literacy, sustainability initiatives, and sector-focused programs, our connect spaces equip individuals, entrepreneurs, and civil society organisations with the tools to grow and thrive. They are safe spaces for collaboration, empowerment, and real solutions to real challenges.

We believe transformation does not start from the top — it starts on the ground. When communities are strengthened, families become resilient, businesses become sustainable, and nations begin to rise.

Our Grassroots Impact Connects are more than spaces.

  • They are places of hope.
  • They are platforms for growth.
  • They are catalysts for lasting change.
