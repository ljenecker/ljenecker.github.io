Business MAP

Sector Enterprise Development

Supporting entrepreneurs and businesses across 21 economic sectors to grow into compliant and sustainable enterprises.

The IEGF Business Market Access Program supports entrepreneurs and businesses across 21 economic sectors to grow into compliant and sustainable enterprises.

The program assists businesses with:

  • Business compliance and formalisation
  • Financial literacy and funding readiness
  • Branding and digital presence
  • Operational systems and infrastructure development
  • Market access and supplier opportunities

Participation in the program is free for IEGF members. Some development services may require additional support depending on the needs of the business.
