IEGF MARKET ACCESS PROGRAM (MAP)

Integrated Economic Development Framework

A Structured Pathway to Economic Stability, Business Growth & Community Transformation

The Market Access Program (MAP) is an 18-month development ecosystem designed to support Individuals, Businesses, Civil Society & Communities through structured, measurable pathways toward economic participation and sustainability.

Explore Programs Access MAP Portal

Three Pillars of Development

MAP addresses economic development holistically through three interconnected participant streams.

Individuals

Employment readiness, personal development, and economic participation.

Businesses

Enterprise growth, sustainability, and sector-aligned development.

Civil Society & Communities

Social impact, governance, and grassroots community development.

Individual MAP: Rise & Thrive Program

Preparing Individuals for the World of Work and Economic Participation

  • Work readiness and employability training
  • Personal financial literacy and financial wellbeing
  • Digital skills and professional development
  • Career pathway guidance and entrepreneurship awareness
  • Personal branding and productivity systems
  • Mentorship and personal growth support

Outcome Participants become work-ready, financially responsible, and equipped to participate meaningfully in the economy.

Business MAP: Sector Enterprise Development

21 Sector-Based Programs Aligned to National SETAs

The IEGF Business MAP delivers structured enterprise development across 21 economic sectors aligned with the SETA framework, supporting entrepreneurs and businesses to grow into compliant, sustainable enterprises.

  • Business compliance and formalisation
  • Financial literacy and funding readiness
  • Branding, digital presence and market positioning
  • Infrastructure and operational systems development
  • Risk management and performance monitoring
  • Supplier and market access opportunities
  • Mentorship and sustainability support

Outcome Businesses become funding-ready, system-driven, and positioned for sustainable growth within their sectors.

21 Sector Enterprise Development Programs

Agriculture, farming, forestry, fisheries and agribusiness

We support businesses to become compliant, improve production systems, access funding, and connect to markets and supply chains for sustainable growth.

Mining, mineral extraction and related support services

We assist businesses with compliance, safety standards, funding readiness, and operational systems to strengthen participation in the mining value chain.

Energy, electricity, renewable energy and water utility

This program supports compliance, infrastructure development, funding access, and positioning businesses within the growing energy and water sectors.

Manufacturing, engineering, automotive and metal industry

We help businesses formalise operations, improve production systems, access funding, and scale within industrial value chains.

Chemical, petroleum, pharmaceutical and plastics

We support compliance, regulatory alignment, funding readiness, and safe operational practices to grow within highly regulated industries.

Food production, catering, and beverage manufacturing

We assist with compliance, food safety standards, funding access, branding, and market entry into formal retail and distribution channels.

Textiles, clothing, furniture, printing and fibre-related

We support product development, branding, compliance, and access to manufacturing and retail opportunities.

Construction, civil works, plumbing and building services

We assist contractors with compliance, certifications, funding readiness, project access, and operational systems to scale sustainably.

Banking, microfinance and financial service providers

We support compliance, financial systems, funding readiness, and strengthening of financial service offerings.

Accounting, auditing, bookkeeping and financial advisory

We assist with professional positioning, compliance, systems, and scaling service-based financial businesses.

Insurance, risk management and advisory services

We support compliance, client management systems, growth strategies, and access to new markets.

ICT, digital services, media, marketing and creative tech

We assist with digital positioning, systems, funding readiness, and scaling within the digital economy.

General services including HR, consulting, cleaning and business services

We support compliance, service structuring, pricing, systems, and growth into sustainable enterprises.

Wholesale, retail, spaza shops and e-commerce

We assist with stock systems, supplier access, funding readiness, and expansion into larger markets.

Transport, logistics, delivery and supply chain

We support compliance, operational systems, fleet management, and access to logistics opportunities.

Businesses supporting government and public service delivery

We assist with compliance, tender readiness, systems, and positioning for public sector opportunities.

Businesses within municipalities and local government services

We support compliance, community-based opportunities, and access to local economic development initiatives.

Security, safety, policing and justice-related services

We assist with compliance, certification, operational systems, and growth within regulated environments.

Training providers, educators and skills development

We support accreditation readiness, compliance, funding access, and program structuring.

Healthcare, wellness, and social development

We assist with compliance, operational systems, funding readiness, and service expansion.

Arts, culture, tourism, hospitality and sports

We support branding, market positioning, funding readiness, and access to tourism and creative industry opportunities.

The Economic Engine

A connected development model where each element strengthens the next.

Why This Model Works

The IEGF MAP creates a connected economic ecosystem:

Individuals gain skills and enter the workforce or start businesses.

Businesses grow and create sustainable jobs.

Communities strengthen and support local economies.

Measurable Results

  • Reduced unemployment
  • Increased business survival rates
  • Stronger local economies
  • Financially stable households
  • Sustainable community development
  • Infrastructure development
