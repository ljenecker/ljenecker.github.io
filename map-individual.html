Rise & Thrive Program

Preparing individuals aged 16 and above for employment, entrepreneurship and economic participation.

The IEGF Individual Market Access Program — Rise & Thrive prepares individuals aged 16 and above for employment, entrepreneurship and economic participation.

Participants receive support in:

  • Work readiness and employability
  • Personal financial literacy and financial wellbeing
  • Digital skills development
  • Entrepreneurship awareness
  • Career development and mentorship

Participation in the program is free for registered IEGF members.
